## 2. The real asset

The technology is replicable. The asset is everything technology is not:

1. 92 universities with deep institutional relationships and active contracts
2. 15 countries of regulatory and operational knowledge
3. A real LATAM skills/competencies dataset (500K+ credentials)
4. Brand recognition (HolonIQ 3x) — credibility that can't be manufactured
5. A specialized implementation consulting team
6. Existing integration footprint inside Canvas and Moodle
7. Combined certification stack (1EdTech + Open Badges 3.0 + blockchain)

Any buyer is buying access to this — not a piece of software.

## 3. Current strategic situation

**Growth has been slow over the last several years.**

**POK (Argentina) is the primary competitive threat.** They offer an unlimited free plan that breaks the standard pricing model, and their public comparison pages contain demonstrably false claims about Acreditta. Result: visible client losses and declining retention. Legal action under Colombia's Ley 256/1996 (competencia desleal) is being evaluated; nothing is filed yet.

**Capital is constrained.** No new investors on the horizon. The EdTech VC market is down ~89% from its 2021 peak. Marketing and sales budget is limited.

**Time matters.** Every quarter without action, POK erodes the customer base and the asset loses value. The window to sell is narrowing, not widening.

---

## 4. Strategic alternatives

**Priority #1: Strategic sale.** Precedents are favorable (Credly $200M to Pearson in 2022; Parchment $795M to Instructure in 2023). The market is actively consolidating. Estimated valuation range: $3–10M, materially higher with competitive tension between two or more buyers.

**Backup if no buyer in 6 months:** Concentrate enterprise-only on Colombia and Mexico, mandatory consulting attached, abandon freemium (where POK wins). Consulting is the moat POK doesn't have.

**Side bets being considered:** Pivot to Credential Strategy-as-a-Service, monetize the dataset as Skills Intelligence, or pursue government registries.
